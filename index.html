What is the WBS?

Nov 08, 2021

Formal definition

WBS stands for Work breakdown structure.

What is really the WBS?

  • The WBS is a structure used to break down or divide the project work to define and better manage and communicate the project scope.

  • The WBS is an abstraction of the project scope.

  • The WBS is not what we have to do! (tasks, processes). Instead is what we have to deliver. Is the answer to the question: What must be delivered to accomplish the project successully?. It presents the end vision, not the means to accomplish it.
